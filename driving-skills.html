Driving Skills & Road Safety

Basic Driving Skills

  • Understanding controls: steering, acceleration, braking, clutch (manual cars).
  • Starting, stopping and reversing safely.
  • Using mirrors effectively and consistently.
  • Lane discipline and signal usage.
  • Defensive driving awareness.

Road Safety Tips

  • Always wear seatbelts.
  • Follow speed limits and adapt to road conditions.
  • Avoid phone use and distractions while driving.
  • Use headlights in poor visibility.
  • Be extra cautious in school zones and residential areas.

Defensive Driving

  • Anticipate other drivers’ actions.
  • Keep safe following distances.
  • Be prepared for unexpected actions by others.
  • Stay calm in traffic — avoid road rage.

Handling Emergencies

  • Know how to react to tire bursts, brake failure or skidding.
  • Use hazard lights to warn other drivers.
  • Pull over safely if the car shows warning signs.
  • Call for roadside help or emergency services when necessary.

Parking Techniques

  • Master parallel parking and reverse parking.
  • Always use parking brakes when parked.
  • Check surroundings before exiting the vehicle.
  • Do not block driveways, walkways or emergency exits.

Driving in Poor Conditions

  • Use fog lights and reduce speed in fog or heavy rain.
  • Keep windshields clean for clear visibility.
  • Increase distance between vehicles during rain or darkness.
  • Slow down during sharp turns or when visibility drops.

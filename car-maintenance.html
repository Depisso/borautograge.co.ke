Car Maintenance Guide

Oil & Fluid Maintenance

  • Change engine oil every 5,000–10,000 km.
  • Monitor brake fluid, coolant, power steering, and transmission fluid.
  • Check for leaks under your car after parking.
  • Ensure oil color isn't too dark — a sign it's dirty.

Tire Inspection & Rotation

  • Check tire pressure weekly and before long trips.
  • Rotate tires every 8,000–10,000 km for even wear.
  • Ensure tires have good tread depth — replace below 1.6mm.
  • Inspect for cracks, bulges, or embedded objects.

Battery Health

  • Clean terminals regularly to avoid corrosion.
  • Check voltage with a multimeter — healthy is around 12.6V.
  • Replace battery every 3–5 years or if frequently discharged.

Brake System Check

  • Listen for squeaking or grinding sounds when braking.
  • Inspect brake pads every 20,000 km.
  • Top up brake fluid if low and inspect for leaks.
  • Replace rotors if excessively worn or warped.

Filters Replacement

  • Replace air filter every 15,000–30,000 km.
  • Change cabin filter annually for cleaner air inside.
  • Fuel filter should be replaced per manufacturer’s interval.

Spark Plugs & Ignition

  • Replace spark plugs every 30,000–100,000 km depending on type.
  • Check for misfires or rough idling as signs of wear.
  • Inspect ignition coils and wires.

Air Conditioning System

  • Run A/C regularly, even in winter, to maintain seals.
  • Recharge refrigerant if cooling weakens.
  • Replace cabin filter to ensure proper airflow.

Belts & Timing Chain

  • Inspect serpentine and timing belts for cracks or frays.
  • Replace belts between 60,000–100,000 km as preventive care.
  • Listen for squealing sounds on startup or sharp turns.

Wipers & Lighting

  • Change wiper blades every 6–12 months.
  • Ensure all exterior and interior lights work — including indicators.
  • Clean lenses for maximum brightness at night.
